Why Nature Matters

Nature provides us with clean air, fresh water, food, and medicine. It regulates our climate, filters our water, and gives us the raw materials that power our economies. Without healthy ecosystems, human life as we know it could not exist.

Biodiversity is the variety of life on Earth and is the foundation of these natural systems. Every plant, animal, fungus, and microorganism plays a role in keeping ecosystems balanced and resilient.

By learning about and appreciating the natural world, we take the first step toward protecting it for future generations. Every small action counts.
